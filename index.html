FLAVOURS OF SOUTHINDIA

South Indian cuisine is known for its bold and diverse flavors. It features **spicy** dishes with the use of red chilies and black pepper, **tangy** notes from tamarind and yogurt, and subtle **sweetness** in desserts like payasam and dishes like appams. **Coconut** is also a common ingredient, providing a nutty, creamy flavor in many curries and chutneys. The cuisine strikes a perfect balance between heat, tanginess, sweetness, and richness, making each dish unique and vibrant.
